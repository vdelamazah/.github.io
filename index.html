FIELDS OF APPLICATION

CONSTRUCTION

FUTURA EUROPE offers a range of polyurethanes and polyureas, dedicated to spray application, specially designed to resolve the major problems in construction, such as: waterproofing of roofs, ceilings, terraces, earth retaining walls, bridges, overpasses, roads, tanks and pipelines (also for drinkable water), pools, asbestos encapsulation, block and prevent of water infiltration, thermal and acoustic insulation.
–

SET DESIGN

FUTURA EUROPE’s offer includes materials with unique technical characteristics, ensuring the right balance between rigidity, flexibility and strength, as well as ensuring a pleasant appearance. The special HARD COAT systems for EPS FUTURA EUROPE are easy to apply, give extreme hardness, dry quickly, are paintable, can be sandpapered and are safe for the environment, they are non-toxic and are odourless.
–
–

INDUSTRY

FUTURA EUROPE offers a wide selection of materials with exceptional characteristics, used in a variety of special applications in industry, such as: abrasion resistant coatings, pipe-line anti corrosion coatings, linings and coatings of tanks containing hydrocarbons, antirust encapsulation, applications for offshore pipelines coatings, coatings of marker and mooring buoys, military security applications and composite construction.
–

SHIPBUILDING

The use of polyureas and polyurethanes in this field is very broad: in addition to the abrasion resistant coating and corrosion protection in shipbuilding, the flexible elastomeric foam are great for lining of boats, ships and yachts seats in order to protect them from satiness and weather.
–
–
–
–

PETROCHEMISTRY

FUTURA EUROPE is the exclusive distributor of polyureas and polyurethanes by U.S. manufacturers guaranteeing the best quality available on the market. These materials, with exceptional characteristics, can be used in the petrochemical industry for pipe linings, pipes, tanks containing hydrocarbons. In addition, our products are among the few ones able to resist unleaded gasoline.
–
–

POLYSTYRENE CLADDING

FUTURA EUROPE offers several product lines for protection and decoration of polystyrene. In addition to polyurethanes, our mortars allow the coating of decorative elements made of polystyrene, with excellent results. The polystirene element assumes hardness and strength (certified by Istituto Giordano) while maintaining some flexibility (controllable depending on your needs). The result is the creation of light yet resistant architectural elements, both for indoor and outdoor, designed to last over time.
